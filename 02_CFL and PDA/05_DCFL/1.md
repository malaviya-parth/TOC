## List of DCFLs

- $a^nb^n$
- $a^{k_1n}b^{k_2n}$
- $a^nb^m| n \gt m$
- $a^nb^m| n \lt m$
- $a^nb^m| n \geq m$
- $a^nb^m| n \leq m$
- $a^nb^m| n = m$
- $a^nb^m| n \neq m$
- $a^{n}b^{3n+2}$
- $w | n_a(w) \neq n_b(w)$
- $a^nb^mc^m|n,m\geq 0$
- $a^nb^mc^n|n,m\geq 0$
- $a^nb^nc^m|n,m\geq 0$
- $a^nb^mc^{n+m}|n,m\geq 0$
- $a^nb^{n+m}c^{m}|n,m\geq 0$
- $a^nb^{n}c^{m}d^m|n,m\geq 0$
- $a^nb^{m}c^{m}d^n|n,m\geq 0$

- $a^nb^n \cup a^nb^{2n}$
- $a^mb^n | m \leq n \leq 2m$
- $a^nb^mc^k | n = m \lor m = k$
- $a^nb^mc^k | n = k \lor m = k$
- $a^nb^mc^k | n = k \lor m = n$
